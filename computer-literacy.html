What is Computer Literacy?

Computer literacy is essentially the knowledge and ability to use a computer efficiently, and just how comfortable one is with operating it. Computer literacy is not the same as computer programming, which focuses on the act of designing and coding programs in computers. You can be very skilled in computer literacy without having to know anything about programming!

Computer literacy can include a variety of technical skills such as understanding operating systems, applications, and basic computer hardware. It also involves the ability to navigate the internet, and other common functions of a computer.

Being such a prevalent tool in our society, being able to operate a computer is a very useful if not required skill to have. There's many intricacies that come with the topic, which is why I've compiled a lot of information about the topic on this website.
